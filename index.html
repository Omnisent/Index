Privacy Notice

What we log — and what we don't

This tool is designed with your privacy in mind. We keep data collection to the bare minimum needed to improve the experience.

We only log the questions and topics users submit — nothing else.

No personal information, account details, session data, or usage patterns are recorded. The questions collected are used solely to understand what topics are most relevant to users.

If you have questions about how this tool handles your information, please reach out to us directly.
